I am a PhD student at University of California, San Diego.

I help machines to learn and perceive the environment through the eyes of a camera.

My research focuses on 3D vision, with a specific emphasis on multi-modal learning for 3D hand/human mesh estimation in the presence of occlusion. Additionally, I am intrigued by video understanding, including action and behavior recognition and localization, and how it intersects with 3D vision. To address these challenges, I aim to develop self-supervised learning algorithms which covers one aspect of my research.
